Emotion Experiment

Deciding how we feel

An Overview:

For one week in 2014, Facebook manipulated over 600,000 Facebook users' news feeds for an experiment about emotions. Half of the users feeds were filled with only positive posts, while the other half saw only negative posts. The users were not aware of this experiment.

Total Affected:

Over 600,000 facebook users

The Fine Print

After receiving some backlash for inducing emotional distress on unknowing participants, Facebook claims that their terms and conditions gives them permission to experiment. See below:

Terms and Conditions:

For example, in addition to helping people see and find things that you do and share, we may use the information we receive about you... for internal operations, including troubleshooting, data analysis, testing, research and service improvement.
