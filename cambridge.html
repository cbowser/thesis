Cambridge Analytica

Taking our data without our consent

An Overview:

The data analytics firm, Cambridge Analytica, paid Facebook users to complete a survey on their app, thisisyourdigitallife. Once the survey was taken, the firm collected the data of the user and the user’s friends, without their knowledge or consent (something that Facebook made possible for apps at the time). The data was used to create psychological profiles, which allowed Cambridge Analytica and the Trump campaign to target Facebook users using a personalized approach.

Total Affected:
